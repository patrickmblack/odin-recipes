Frozen Grapes

Description

Frozen grapes. Great low-calorie snack for the warmer season.

Ingredients

  • Grapes

Steps

  1. Wash grapes and dry very very well
  2. Put grapes in freezer-safe container
  3. Freeze grapes for at least 4 hours, overnight is best
  4. Extra: Sprinkle some citric acid to make them like healthy sour skittles.
