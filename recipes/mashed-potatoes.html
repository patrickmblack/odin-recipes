Mashed Potatoes

Description

Mashed potatoes are the perfect carb side dish for any main course. Potatoes, butter, salt, and a dream.

Ingredients

  • 2 Russet potatoes per person. Washed throughly
  • A large pot of boiling salted water
  • Cream / Half and half / milk
  • Butter
  • Salt, to taste

Steps

  1. Bring a large pot of water boiling. Add a TBSP of salt
  2. Througohly wash the potatoes. Peel if youd like, I prefer them rustic with the peel
  3. Cut the potatoes into smaller chunks, each chunk of equal size. I prefer into 8ths
  4. Boil the potatoes for 10-15 minutes. Poke with fork to test doneness. If they resist or don't break apart, continue boiling
  5. Drain water, add potatoes back to pot
  6. Potato masher them into as fine of a mash as you prefer
  7. Add the milk or cream to your desired thickness and flavor. I prefer no more than a cup of milk
  8. Add some butter
  9. Serve!
