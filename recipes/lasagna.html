Lasagna

Description

Who doesn't love a nice tray of lasagna? Carbs, cheese, meat, tomatoes. Yum!

The first time I ever had lasagna I was 7 years old living in the country side of Venezuela...

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion and Garlic
  • Tomato products
  • Sugar
  • Spices and seasonings
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Cheeses
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Make the ricotta mixture
  4. Layer the lasagna in order of meat sauce, then noodles, then ricotta mixture. Repeat
  5. Cover with foil and bake at 375f for 25 minutes. Uncover and continue baking for 25 additional minutes.
  6. Let the lasagna rest.
  7. Enjoy.
